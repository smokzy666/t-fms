Our Core Speciality

Pharmaceutical Security

02

Denmark is home to one of Europe's most concentrated pharmaceutical and life sciences clusters. The corridor from Copenhagen through Ballerup to Kalundborg houses global companies including Novo Nordisk, Leo Pharma, Lundbeck, and Genmab — all operating GMP-regulated facilities that demand a security partner who understands their environment.

T-FMS Security Group has worked within pharmaceutical manufacturing, R&D, and logistics environments since our founding in 2008. We are familiar with GMP zoning requirements, clean-room access protocols, material segregation, and the audit trail documentation that regulated facilities require from their security providers.

Our teams are trained on pharmaceutical site induction requirements and can operate within ATEX, clean-room, and controlled-temperature environments without disrupting production or compliance processes.

Company History

03

T-FMS Security Group was founded in 2008 by a team of former law enforcement and military professionals with one purpose: to deliver security that performs under real-world pressure.

What began as a specialist consultancy in Copenhagen has grown into a full-spectrum security firm with deep expertise in pharmaceutical, financial, and critical infrastructure sectors across Denmark, Sweden, and Norway.

We are ISO 9001 certified and operate under strict quality management principles. Our staff undergo continuous training, background verification, and sector-specific induction before deployment.
